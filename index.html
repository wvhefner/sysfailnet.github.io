About

Security professional operating at the intersection of cybersecurity, intelligence, and human-centric security.

With a foundation in both technical systems and strategic analysis, I focus on understanding and countering adversarial behavior across digital, cognitive, and biological domains. My work blends hands-on technical practice with academic research — building the kind of depth that static checklists and compliance frameworks never capture.

I hold a CISSP and multiple GIAC certifications, and I am currently completing a Master of Arts in Intelligence and Security Studies at Augusta University.
